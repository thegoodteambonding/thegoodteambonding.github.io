About Us

We love team bonding. We love animals.

Why not do both?

At The Good Team Bonding, we work with our partners to create activities and workshops for you and your team.

As a company, you pay the same price as you would externally, but through The Good Team Bonding, 10% of what you pay goes to an animal welfare charity.

90% of what you pay go to our partners, 10% go to animal welfare charity. We do not take any revenues or profits.

How does it work?

Drop us a message

Tell us the kind of team bonding activity you are interested in, how many pax, the dates you are looking at etc.

Get a quote from us

We do not charge companies extra by going through The Good Team Bonding. Partners working with us agree to take 90% of the fees, and give 10% to animal welfare charities.

Show up and have fun!

This is optional, but we include a 30-minute educational talk by an Animal Welfare Group (AWG) -depends on availability.

Frequently Asked Questions

If your question is not answered here, feel free to drop us a message!

  • 01 Why should I choose The Good Team Bonding?

    1. We have really awesome activities, specially curated by us!
    2. We DO NOT mark up our prices, so the rates of our activities are the same as that you will find outside
    3. You get to give back to Animal Welfare Groups (AWGs), and who doesn't love animals?

  • 02 Do you get a cut of the profit/fees?

    No, we don't. 90% of the fees you pay for the team bonding activities will go to our partners, and 10% will go to Animal Welfare Groups (AWGs).

  • 03 Why are you doing this?

    During my free time, I volunteer for Animal Welfare Groups (AWGs) and I've seen how hard they work to help every rescued animal.

    The donations they get will definitely go a long way to helping them pay for food and veterinary bills.

About me

Stacy Goh

Founder

Howdy everyone! My name is Stacy, I'm a software engineer by day and a animal outreach volunteer whenever I have free time.

I'm passionate about education and strongly believe in educating the public on pet care.

I started this to make a small financial contribution to Animal Welfare Groups (AWGs) to help cover some of their medical costs and to bring awareness on the animal welfare issues in Singapore.
